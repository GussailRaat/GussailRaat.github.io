**Duration:** Jul 2018 -- Jun 2020 | **Funded by:** SkyMap Global Pte Ltd, Singapore

This project aimed to identify client intentions toward closing deals by analyzing their interest levels. By leveraging advanced sentiment analysis techniques, the project focused on interpreting communication patterns to determine whether a client was inclined to move forward with a deal or exhibited hesitation. This work aimed to provide actionable insights for businesses, enabling them to enhance decision-making, refine client targeting, and improve negotiation outcomes.
