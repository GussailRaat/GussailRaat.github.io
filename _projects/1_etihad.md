**Duration:** Sep 2025 -- Present

Leading an applied research collaboration between MBZUAI and **Etihad Airways** on the development of a **multilingual conversational chatbot** for flight booking, customer support, and other aviation services. The work spans LLM fine-tuning, retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) over airline knowledge bases, and grounding for factual reliability.
