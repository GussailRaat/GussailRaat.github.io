**Duration:** Jul 2020 -- Jun 2023 | **Funded by:** Ministry of Home Affairs, India

This project has two modules:

**Module 1 -- Multimodal and Multilingual Sentiment and Emotion Analysis System for Victims:** A system designed to tackle cybersecurity challenges, offering a sophisticated approach to detect cybercrime effectively. The system uses sentiment and emotion analysis to better understand user interactions, improving threat detection and providing a stronger defense against cybercrime in diverse digital environments.

**Module 2 -- AI-based Interactive Counseling Chatbot for Victims:** An AI-based interactive counseling chatbot system designed to provide support to victims. Through this initiative, victims can access empathetic and responsive assistance, fostering a sense of comfort and understanding.
