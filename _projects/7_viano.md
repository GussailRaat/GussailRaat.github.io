**Duration:** Jul 2013 -- Jun 2014 | **B.Tech Project, MIET Meerut**

This project aimed to create VIANO, a Laser-Projected Virtual Piano that turns any surface into an interactive musical interface. By leveraging laser technology, VIANO offers a portable and immersive way for musicians and enthusiasts to play music without a physical instrument, redefining musical expression in diverse settings.
