Dushyant Singh Chauhan

Postdoctoral Research Associate at MBZUAI, Abu Dhabi, UAE

MBZUAI, Masdar City

Abu Dhabi, UAE

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate at Mohamed bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence (MBZUAI), UAE, working under the supervision of Dr. Nils Lukas. My current research focuses on reasoning models, privacy and security of large language models (LLMs), and multimodal analysis. I am also working on an industry project with Etihad Airways on conversational AI chatbots.

I completed my doctoral studies at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Patna, specializing in Artificial Intelligence, Natural Language Processing, and Computer Vision, under the guidance of Prof. Asif Ekbal (IIT Jodhpur) and Prof. Pushpak Bhattacharyya (IIT Bombay). My doctoral research focused on Investigating Multimodal Analysis and Fusion Techniques, including multimodal sentiment and emotion analysis, sarcasm detection and cause extraction, causal reasoning, video summarization, multimodal LLMs for enhanced response generation and chatbot development, multimodal RAG systems, and more.

Google Scholar Citations shows more than 900 career citations, with an h-index of 12 and an i10-index of 14.
